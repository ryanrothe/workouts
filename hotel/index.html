Key technique cues

  • Pull-up waves (2/3/5): 2 reps, short rest, 3 reps, short rest, 5 reps — that's 1 wave. Rest 60–90s, then next wave. Total: 70 reps across 21 mini-sets.
  • Goblet squat tempo: 6 sec down, 6 sec pause at bottom, drive up at normal speed. Each rep ~13–15 sec. Pick a weight you can hold through all 50 reps with form intact — usually lighter than you'd guess.
  • Lateral / side raise superset: Lateral raise straight into side raise without rest, then rest before the next round.
  • If weights are too light: Swap in single-leg variations (single-leg goblet, single-leg RDL). Bulgarians are already unilateral.
