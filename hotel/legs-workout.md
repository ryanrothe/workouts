## Tempo Breakdown — Goblet Squat

- **6 sec down** (eccentric)
- **6 sec hold** (pause at bottom)
- Drive up at normal speed

Each rep is ~13–15 seconds. A set of 10 ≈ 2:30 of time-under-tension. Pick a weight you can hold through all 50 reps with form intact — that usually means lighter than you'd guess.
