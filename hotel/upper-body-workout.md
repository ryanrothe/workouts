**Focus:** Push / Pull / Shoulders / Arms
**Source:** Voxer message, Wed 7:43 AM

## Workout

| # | Exercise | Sets × Reps | Notes |
|---|----------|-------------|-------|
| 1 | Plyo Push-Ups | 10 × 3 | 60 sec rest between sets |
| 2 | Max Rep Push-Ups | AMRAP — 5 minutes | One continuous block; rest as needed within the 5 min |
| 3 | DB High Incline Press | 3 × max reps | Heaviest DBs possible |
| 4 | DB Lateral Raise | 3 × 15 | Strict form |
| 4a | DB Side Raise | 3 × 15 | Superset with #4 |
| 5 | Pull-Ups (Wave: 2 / 3 / 5) | 7 waves | 1 wave = 2 reps → 3 reps → 5 reps. Complete 7 waves total. |
| 6 | DB Hammer Curl | 4 × 6 | Heavy, controlled |

---

## Notes

- **Wave loading (Exercise 5):** Each wave is three mini-sets — 2 reps, then 3 reps, then 5 reps — with short rest between the mini-sets and a longer rest before the next wave. Total pull-up volume: **70 reps** across 21 mini-sets.
- **Superset (4 / 4a):** Lateral raise straight into side raise without rest, then rest before the next round.
- **AMRAP push-ups:** Goal is max total reps in 5 minutes. Break the set up however you need to keep moving.

---

## Quick Totals

- Push volume: 30 plyo + AMRAP + 3 sets max-rep incline press
- Shoulder volume: 90 reps (lateral + side raises)
- Pull volume: 70 reps (pull-up waves)
- Arm volume: 24 reps hammer curls
